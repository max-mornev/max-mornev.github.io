How to get here

EPFL provides a detailed map of the campus:

From EPFL Metro station: Take the overpass across the platforms and the street (Route de la Sorge). Follow Route de la Sorge and turn right to a small park with a bicycle repair facility (Point Velo). Follow the path alongside avenue Auguste-Piccard to an elevated walkway. At the end of the walkway take the staircase to the next floor. Turn left, walk a bit and enter the door of the mathematics department. Take the staircase to the next floor, turn left and follow the long corridor all the way to a hall. My office on your right.
