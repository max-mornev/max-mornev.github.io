Maxim Mornev

I am a post-doc at the Instituto de Ciencias Matemáticas in Madrid working in the group of Daniel Macias. My research area is number theory, more specifically, Drinfeld modules and shtukas.

Between 2018 and 2025 I was a post-doc with Richard Pink at ETH Zürich and with Maryna Viazovska at EPFL. I obtained my PhD degree from Universiteit Leiden and Università degli Studi di Milano in 2018.
